# Quick Start Guide

We're excited to help you integrate Venly's blockchain technology into your applications. Our APIs and SDKs allow your users to interact easily with blockchain networks without requiring them to manage their own wallets or understand the underlying technology.

This comprehensive documentation hub is your ultimate resource for all things Venly. Whether you're a seasoned blockchain developer or embarking on your journey, you'll discover all the information you need to kickstart your integration with Venly's products and services.

We've got you covered, from detailed API reference guides to step-by-step tutorials. We're excited to see what you'll create with Venly, so let's dive in and start building!

---

## Account Registration

To get started, you can create an account in our [Developer Portal](https://portal.venly.io/?utm_source=website&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=General+-+Venly+Internal) to gain access to Venly's suite of products.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit the [Developer Portal](https://portal.venly.io/?utm_source=website&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=General+-+Venly+Internal) and register using your email address or take advantage of the multiple social login options available.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Enter a name for your workspace and continue. A **14-day Platform Trial** is automatically created, and you instantly get access to the Portal and all sandbox/production services.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Building">
    Access your dashboard and begin exploring Venly's APIs and services immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How to Connect

Find your access credentials on the [Authentication](https://portal.venly.io/authentication/?utm_source=website&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=General+-+Venly+Internal) page in the [Portal](https://portal.venly.io/?utm_source=website&utm_medium=docs&utm_campaign=General+-+Venly+Internal).

### Authentication Credentials

There are two types of access credentials available:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sandbox Credentials" icon="flask">
    For the **sandbox environment** used for testing on testnet chains.
    
    **API Services use:**
    - Client ID
    - Client Secret
    
    **Widget uses:**
    - Client ID
    - Whitelisted URLs
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Production Credentials" icon="server">
    For the **production environment** used for deploying on mainnet chains.
    
    **API Services use:**
    - Client ID
    - Client Secret
    
    **Widget uses:**
    - Client ID
    - Whitelisted URLs
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
Use the toggle in the top right corner of the Portal to switch between your **Sandbox** and **Production** access credentials.
</Tip>

## Ready to Play with Testnet Assets?

As soon as you sign up with Venly, we create for you a **testnet user** and a **MATIC wallet** in the sandbox environment with the following digital assets automatically deposited:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="1 POL Token" icon="coins">
    Native token for testing transactions
  </Card>
  <Card title="100 Venly Test Tokens" icon="circle-dollar">
    ERC20 tokens for testing fungible token operations
  </Card>
  <Card title="1 NFT" icon="image">
    Non-fungible token for testing NFT functionality
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### What Can You Test?

Upon activation, you can immediately explore functionalities such as:

- ✅ Wallet creation and management
- ✅ Message signing
- ✅ Token transfers (native and ERC20)
- ✅ NFT transfers
- ✅ Creating signing methods for new users
- ✅ Smart contract interactions
