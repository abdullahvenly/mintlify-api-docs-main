Introducing the **[Faucet](https://portal.venly.io/faucet)**, built into our [Developer Portal](https://portal.venly.io/) can be used to request testnet **MATIC** and **AVALANCHE** tokens. You can request **0.5 MATIC** and **0.5 AVAX** tokens every 24 hours using wallets created with **Venly**.

## Polygon & Avalanche Faucet

The Faucet feature in Venly's Developer Portal allows users to request **MATIC** and **AVALANCHE** tokens for testing purposes on their respective testnets.

### Available Testnet Tokens

When you use Venly's Faucet feature, you can request the following every **24 hours**:

  <Card title="Polygon Amoy" icon="hexagon">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Avalanche Fuji" icon="mountain">
    0.5 AVAX tokens every 24 hours
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How Does the Faucet Work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Developer Portal">
    Go to Venly's [Developer Portal](https://portal.venly.io/) and navigate to **Wallet API**
    
    <Warning>
    Make sure you are under **Sandbox**. Click the toggle to change from Production to Sandbox.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Token">
    Choose whether you want to request **MATIC** or **AVALANCHE** tokens
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Wallet Details">
    Enter your **wallet address** or **wallet UUID** associated with Venly. This ensures that the tokens are sent to the correct wallet
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request Tokens">
    Click on the "Request tokens" button to submit your request. Tokens will be sent to your wallet shortly after
  </Step>
</Steps>
