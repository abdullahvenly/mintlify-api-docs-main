We are excited to announce the launch of the new **NFT-Insights** feature in the Venly Portal. This update provides users with powerful tools to view, search, and manage their NFT contracts, token-types, and tokens.

## Configuration Tab

The existing components (webhooks) currently displayed on the NFT-API page are organized under a new **Configuration** tab.

## New Features under the Contracts Tab

### 1. View All NFT Contracts

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Complete Visibility" icon="eye">
    Gain complete visibility of all your NFT contracts across both production and sandbox environments
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Easy Navigation" icon="map">
    Easily navigate through your contracts for better asset management
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
Switch between the environments using the toggle button on top right
</Tip>

### 2. Search NFT Contracts

Search capabilities allow you to locate specific NFT contracts using:

- **Contract Address**: Enter a specific contract address to find your NFT contract (partial search also works)
- **Contract Name**: Search by name for faster results
- **Sorting**: Optionally sort your NFT contracts by name in ascending or descending order by clicking on the arrow next to **NAME**

### 3. Detailed NFT Contract Information

Access comprehensive information about individual NFT contracts, including:

- Contract Metadata
- Royalty configuration details
- List of all token-types under the contract
