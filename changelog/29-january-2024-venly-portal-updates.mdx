Revamp of the **Company Settings** page in the Developer Portal plus ability to add your company's VAT number.

## Company Settings

Revamp of the **[company settings](https://portal.venly.io/company-settings)** page with ability to add billing details such as:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Location Details" icon="map-location-dot">
    - Country
    - City
    - State/Province
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Address Details" icon="address-card">
    - Postal/ZIP Code
    - Street Address
    - VAT Number
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## VAT Number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add VAT Number">
    You can add your VAT number from the company settings page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Format Requirements">
    Venly does not validate the VAT number, so make sure you enter the correct VAT number in the correct format
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Without Country Code">
    Make sure to enter the VAT number without the country code
  </Step>
</Steps>
