Quick update on the **PAY** fees per subscription plan.

## Updated Fee Structure per Plan

**PAY** deducts a **small percentage fee** on every successful transaction as follows:

| Plan | Fee Structure |
|:-----|:--------------|
| **Developer** | 4.99% + $0.75/tx |
| **Professional** | 4.99% + $0.75/tx |
| **Enterprise** | 4.99% + $0.5/tx |

<Info>
For more fee information, view the [pricing plans](https://www.venly.io/pricing).
</Info>
