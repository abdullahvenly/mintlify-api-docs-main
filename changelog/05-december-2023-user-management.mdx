### Key Features

- Invite **new** and **existing users** to join your company via **email**
- Invitation links are valid for **10 days**
- Your users can **accept** or **decline** the invitation
- **Access credentials** for all your subscribed products are shared amongst your company's users
- Ability to **delete a user** from your company
- Ability to **invite users again** if:
  - The invitation link is expired
  - The user declines the invitation
  - The user is deleted
- Ability to **resend invite** (if somehow the user does not receive invitation email)
- Ability to **delete an invitation**
- View the status of the invitation if it's **accepted**, **declined**, or **invited**
- **Admin rights** are the same amongst **all users** of your company

## How to Invite a User?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Head over to the [dashboard of Portal](https://portal.venly.io/overview)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Users">
    Click on **Users**
    
    <Frame caption="Venly Business Portal">
      <img src="https://files.readme.io/485e258-image.png" alt="Portal Dashboard" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Invite User">
    Click on **Invite user** on the top right. A pop-up will appear. Enter the **email** of the user you want to invite and click on **Invite user**
    
    <Frame caption="Invite user by email">
      <img src="https://files.readme.io/043e560-image.png" alt="Invite User Form" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    A small pop-up will appear on the top right informing you the user was invited **successfully**. You can also see the **status** of the invitation and the **validity** of the invite
    
    <Frame caption="User Invited Successfully">
      <img src="https://files.readme.io/e4dfc89-image.png" alt="Success Notification" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invitation Email Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="User Receives Email">
    Once your user receives the invitation email, they need to click on **Accept Invitation**
    
    <Frame caption="Invitation Email">
      <img src="https://files.readme.io/7e398ac-image.png" alt="Email Invitation" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Redirect to Portal">
    They will get redirected to the **Portal log-in** page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Login or Sign Up">
    - An **existing user** can just log in with their email address and accept/decline the invitation
    - A **new user** will be prompted to create an account on Portal. Thereafter, they can accept or decline the invitation
  </Step>
