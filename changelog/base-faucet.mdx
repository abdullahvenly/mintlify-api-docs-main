The **[Faucet](https://portal.venly.io/faucet)**, built into our [Developer Portal](https://portal.venly.io/) can now be used to request testnet **BASE** test tokens. You can request **0.1 ETH** tokens on the BASE Sepolia testnet, every 24 hours using wallets created with **Venly**.

## BASE Faucet

The [Faucet](https://portal.venly.io/faucet) now allows users to request **BASE** tokens for testing purposes on the BASE Sepolia testnet blockchain.

### Available Testnet Tokens

When you use the Faucet, you can request the following test tokens every **24 hours**:

## How Does the Faucet Work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Faucet">
    Go to Venly's [Developer Portal](https://portal.venly.io/) and navigate to **[Faucet](https://portal.venly.io/faucet)**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Token">
    Choose whether you want to request **BASE**, **MATIC**, or **AVALANCHE** tokens
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Wallet Details">
    Enter your **wallet address** or **wallet UUID** associated with Venly. This ensures that the tokens are sent to the correct wallet
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Request Tokens">
    Click on the "Request tokens" button to submit your request. Tokens will be sent to your wallet shortly after
  </Step>
</Steps>
