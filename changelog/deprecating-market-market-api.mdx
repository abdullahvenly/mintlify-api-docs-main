The **Venly Market** and the **Market-API** will be decommissioned on **August 31st, 2024**. Please ensure you withdraw any remaining funds before this date. Any open listings will automatically be canceled on the 31st of August.

## Why Are We Sunsetting These Products?

Due to regulations, we have decommissioned the **[Market](https://venly.market/home)** and **[Market-API](https://docs.venly.io/docs/market-api-overview)**. Venly offers non-custodial services while the Market and Market-API are custodial. This is why we chose to sunset these products.

## Deadline

<Warning>
**Venly Market and Market-API will be decommissioned on August 31st, 2024.**
</Warning>
