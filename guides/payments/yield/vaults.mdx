## Understanding APY

**APY = Annual Percentage Yield**

Shows expected return if you kept funds deposited for one year with compound interest.

### Important Notes

- APY changes based on market conditions
- Displayed APY is current rate
- Past performance doesn't guarantee future results
- Actual returns may vary

<Tip>
  Check APY regularly as rates fluctuate with supply and demand.
</Tip>

## Understanding TVL

**TVL = Total Value Locked**

Shows total amount deposited across all users.

### What It Indicates

- **High TVL**: More trust, proven track record
- **Low TVL**: Newer or less tested
- **Growing TVL**: Increasing adoption
- **Shrinking TVL**: Users withdrawing

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I lose my principal?">
    Yes, it's possible but rare. Risks include:
    - Smart contract bugs
    - Protocol hacks
    - Extreme market events
    
    Choose audited protocols and diversify to minimize risk.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is interest calculated?">
    Interest accrues continuously based on current APY. It compounds automatically in most vaults.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw anytime?">
    Generally yes, but some vaults may have:
    - Short lock periods
    - Withdrawal processing time
    - Liquidity constraints during high demand
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if APY drops?">
    APY fluctuates naturally. If it drops significantly:
    - You can withdraw anytime
    - Move to different vault
    - Wait for rates to recover
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
