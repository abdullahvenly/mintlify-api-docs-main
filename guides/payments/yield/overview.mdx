## How It Works

1. Choose a vault (low, medium, or high risk)
2. Deposit stablecoins (USDC, USDT, DAI)
3. Earn returns automatically
4. Withdraw anytime

## Risk Levels

**Low Risk (4-6% APY):** Aave, Compound - safest protocols

**Medium Risk (6-8% APY):** Curve, Yearn - balanced approach

**High Risk (8%+ APY):** Convex - aggressive strategies

<Warning>
  Higher returns = higher risks. Only invest what you can afford to lose.
</Warning>

## Fees

- **Deposit/Withdrawal:** Free
- **Performance Fee:** 10-15% of earned interest only

## Security
