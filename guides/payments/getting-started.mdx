## Step 1: Create Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Invitation Code">
    Visit the registration page and enter your invitation code from the email.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Credentials">
    - Email: Your business email
    - Password: Minimum 8 characters (strong password recommended)
    - Confirm password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up 2FA">
    1. Download Google Authenticator
    2. Scan the QR code
    3. Enter the 6-digit code
    
    <Tip>
      Save your 2FA backup code securely
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Business Verification

Your account enters verification (1-3 business days). During this time, you can explore the dashboard and add accounts, but cannot create transactions.

[Learn more about verification →](/guides/payments/onboarding)

## Step 3: Add Bank Accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Accounts">
    Go to **Accounts** → **Bank Accounts**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Whitelist Account">
    Enter: Account name, IBAN, Bank name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Click the verification link sent to your email
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Add Crypto Wallets

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Create New (Recommended)">
    1. Click **Create New Wallet**
    2. Choose wallet type and blockchain
    3. Wallet created instantly
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Whitelist Existing">
    1. Click **Whitelist Wallet**
    2. Enter wallet address and network
    3. Send exactly 1 USDC to verify ownership
    4. Wait for confirmation (5-15 minutes)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
