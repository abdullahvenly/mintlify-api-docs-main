## Onboarding Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Receive Onboarding Email">
    You'll receive an email with an onboarding link from Venly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="2. Set Your Password">
    Click the link and create a strong password for your account
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="3. Configure 2FA">
    Set up two-factor authentication using any authenticator app
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="4. Account in Processing">
    After logging in, your account will be in "processing" state
    
    **Can do**: Whitelist bank accounts and crypto wallets
    
    **Cannot do**: Create transactions until verification is complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="5. Account Verified">
    Once verified, you'll have full access to create transactions
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## During Processing: Whitelist Accounts

While your account is being verified, you can prepare by adding accounts:

### Whitelist Bank Accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Go to **Accounts** ‚Üí **Bank Accounts** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Details">
    Enter your IBAN, bank name, and account name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manual Review">
    Your bank account will be reviewed by the Venly team
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Whitelist Crypto Wallets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Go to **Accounts** ‚Üí **Wallets** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Address">
    Enter your wallet address and select the network
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Ownership">
    You'll see a verification screen showing the amount and address
    
    Send exactly 1 USDC from your wallet to the provided verification address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Verification will complete automatically
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You must send 1 USDC from the exact wallet address you're registering
</Warning>
