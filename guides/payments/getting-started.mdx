## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Invitation Code">
    Visit the registration page and enter your invitation code. This code was sent to your business email.
    
    <Tip>
      Make sure to enter the code exactly as shown (uppercase). The code format is: `VNLYRCKS25`
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Account Credentials">
    Create your login credentials:
    - **Email**: Your business email address
    - **Password**: Minimum 8 characters (use a strong password)
    - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
    
    <Warning>
      Use a strong password combining letters, numbers, and special characters for better security.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set Up 2FA">
    Secure your account with two-factor authentication:
    
    1. Download Google Authenticator on your mobile device
    2. Scan the QR code displayed on screen
    3. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app
    
    <Note>
      If you can't scan the QR code, use the manual entry code provided below the QR code.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Business Verification

After creating your account, your business will go through a verification process.

### What Happens During Verification?

Your account status will show **"Application in Process"** for 1-3 business days while we verify:

- Business registration details
- Compliance requirements
- Identity verification

### During the Verification Period

✅ **You CAN:**
- Access the dashboard
- Add bank accounts and wallets
- Review fee structures
- Explore available features

❌ **You CANNOT:**
- Create transactions (on-ramp/off-ramp)
- Deposit in yield vaults
- Process payments

<Info>
  You'll receive an email notification once your business is approved. The dashboard will display an "Approved Business" badge when ready.
</Info>

## Step 3: Add Your Accounts

Once your business is approved, set up your payment accounts:

### Add Bank Accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Accounts">
    Go to **Accounts** → **Bank Accounts** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click 'Whitelist Bank Account'">
    Enter your bank details:
    - Account Name (e.g., "Business Checking")
    - IBAN
    - Bank Name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify via Email">
    Check your email and click the verification link. Your account will be active once verified.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Add Crypto Wallets

You have two options:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Create New Wallet">
    **Let Venly create a managed wallet for you:**
    
    1. Click **"Create New Wallet"**
    2. Choose wallet type (Business/Treasury/Payout)
    3. Select blockchain network
    4. Wallet is instantly created and active
    
    <Tip>
      Managed wallets are immediately active and ready to use.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Whitelist Existing Wallet">
    **Add your own wallet (e.g., MetaMask):**
    
    1. Click **"Whitelist Wallet"**
    2. Enter wallet name and address
    3. Select blockchain network
    4. Send exactly 1 USDC to the verification address
    5. Wait for blockchain confirmation
    
    <Warning>
      You must send exactly 1 USDC from the wallet you're registering to verify ownership.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Quick Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
    Business verification typically takes 1-3 business days. You'll receive an email once approved.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I add multiple bank accounts?">
    Yes! You can add multiple bank accounts and crypto wallets. Each must be verified separately.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I lose access to my 2FA device?">
    Contact our support team immediately at support@venly.io for account recovery assistance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are there transaction limits?">
    Transaction limits depend on your verification level and account history. Contact support for specific limits.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
