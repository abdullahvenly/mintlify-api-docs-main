## What is an On-Ramp?

An on-ramp converts fiat currency (EUR, USD, GBP) from your bank account into cryptocurrency in your wallet.

```
Your Bank Account → Venly → Your Crypto Wallet
```

<Info>
  Processing time: 1-2 hours after your bank transfer is confirmed
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before creating an on-ramp:

✅ Business approved (green badge)
✅ Verified bank account
✅ Active crypto wallet
✅ Sufficient funds in bank account

## After Creating the Transaction

### Transaction Status Updates

Your transaction will progress through these stages:

**1. Transaction Created** (Blue)
- Initial status after creation
- Waiting for your bank transfer
- **Your action**: Complete the bank transfer

**2. Waiting for Funds** (Amber)
- Transfer initiated from your side
- Platform monitoring for receipt
- **Your action**: Wait for bank confirmation (can take up to 1 hour)

**3. Processing** (Purple)
- Payment received and confirmed
- Converting fiat to crypto
- Sending crypto to your wallet
- **Your action**: None - wait for completion

**4. Completed** (Green)
- Crypto successfully sent to your wallet
- Transaction finished
- **Your action**: Verify crypto in your wallet

### Email Notifications

You'll receive emails at each stage:
- Transaction created (with bank instructions)
- Payment received
- Processing started
- Crypto sent to wallet

## Processing Timeline

```
Minute 0    - Create transaction
Minute 0-60 - Complete your bank transfer
Minute 5-90 - Bank confirms transfer
Minute 60-120 - Crypto sent to wallet
```

<Tip>
  Bank transfers during business hours (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 5 PM) process faster than those made outside these hours.
</Tip>

## Tips for Successful On-Ramps

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Always include the reference code">
    The reference code (ON-XXXXXXXX) is essential. Without it, we cannot automatically match your payment and processing will be delayed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transfer exact amount">
    Transfer the exact amount shown. If you send a different amount, it may cause processing delays or rejection.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use the correct bank account">
    Transfer from the bank account you selected during transaction creation. Transfers from different accounts may be rejected.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Save your bank receipt">
    Keep proof of your bank transfer. This helps resolve any issues quickly.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Check transaction status">
    Monitor your transaction status in the platform. You'll see updates as processing progresses.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Bank transfer not detected">
    **Wait Time**: Up to 2 hours for detection
    
    **Check**:
    - Did you include the reference code?
    - Did you send the exact amount?
    - Is the transfer from the correct bank account?
    
    **Solution**: Contact support with:
    - Transaction ID
    - Bank transfer receipt
    - Transfer reference/confirmation number
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong amount transferred">
    **If you sent less**: Contact support to:
    - Cancel transaction and refund
    - OR process with adjusted amount
    
    **If you sent more**: Contact support
    - Excess will be refunded or credited
    - Processing may be delayed
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Forgot reference code">
    **Critical**: Without the reference code, automatic matching fails
    
    **Solution**:
    1. Contact support immediately
    2. Provide transaction ID
    3. Provide bank transfer proof
    4. Manual matching will be attempted
    5. Processing will be delayed by 1-2 business days
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transaction stuck in 'Waiting for Funds'">
    **Reasons**:
    - Bank transfer not yet sent
    - Bank transfer still pending confirmation
    - Reference code missing or incorrect
    - Amount mismatch
    
    **Solution**:
    1. Verify you completed the bank transfer
    2. Check bank transfer status with your bank
    3. Wait full 2 hours before contacting support
    4. Contact support@venly.io if still stuck
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Test First**: Start with a small amount (€100-500) for your first on-ramp to familiarize yourself with the process.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Business Hours**: Initiate transactions during banking hours (Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 5 PM) for faster processing.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Double Check**: Review all details twice before confirming, especially the reference code inclusion in your bank transfer.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Once you transfer funds from your bank, the transaction cannot be cancelled. Ensure all details are correct before transferring.
</Warning>
