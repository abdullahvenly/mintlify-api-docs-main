## What is an On-Ramp?

Convert fiat (EUR, USD, GBP) from your bank → cryptocurrency in your wallet

**Processing Time:** 1-2 hours after bank transfer confirmation

## Prerequisites

✅ Business approved\
✅ Verified bank account\
✅ Active crypto wallet\
✅ Funds in bank account

![On Ramp Flow Pn](/images/on-ramp-flow.png)

## Creating an On-Ramp

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    Dashboard → **Create On-Ramp** or Transactions → **New On-Ramp**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Amount & Currencies">
    - Amount: Minimum €50
    - From: EUR, USD, or GBP
    - To: USDC, ETH, MATIC, etc.

    Review: Estimated crypto amount, exchange rate, fees
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Accounts">
    - Source: Your bank account
    - Destination: Your crypto wallet
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review & Confirm">
    Double-check all details, then click **Confirm & Create**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Bank Transfer">
    **Critical Information Displayed:**

    - Beneficiary: PayRamp Solutions B.V.
    - IBAN: (exact IBAN shown)
    - Amount: (exact amount)
    - **Reference Code**: `ON-ABC12345` ⚠️ **MUST INCLUDE**

    Complete the transfer from your bank with this reference code
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  **The reference code is CRITICAL.** Your payment cannot be matched without it. Include it in the payment description/reference field.
</Warning>

## After Creating

Your transaction progresses through these stages:

1. **Transaction Created** → Complete bank transfer
2. **Waiting for Funds** → Wait for bank confirmation (up to 1 hour)
3. **Processing** → Platform processing (30-60 min)
4. **Completed** → Crypto in your wallet

## Fees Example

€5,000 EUR → USDC:

- Base: €5,000
- Processing (0.5%): €25
- Tier 1 (0.45%): €22.50
- **Total fees: €47.50**
- You receive: ~5,053 USDC

## Tips for Success

<Tip>
  **Always include reference code** - Without it, processing delays by 1-2 days
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Transfer exact amount** - Different amounts cause delays
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Use correct bank account** - Must match the account selected
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

**Transfer not detected after 2 hours?**

- Included reference code? ✅
- Sent exact amount? ✅
- From correct account? ✅
- Contact support with transaction ID + bank receipt

**Forgot reference code?**

- Contact support immediately with transaction ID and bank receipt
- Manual matching attempted (1-2 day delay)
