## What Are Transactions?

Transactions enable you to convert between fiat currency and cryptocurrency.

## Transaction Flow

### On-Ramp Process

```
Bank Account → Platform → Crypto Wallet
```

1. Create transaction request
2. Receive bank transfer instructions
3. Complete bank transfer
4. System processes payment (1-2 hours)
5. Crypto arrives in your wallet

**Total Time**: 1-2 hours after bank transfer

### Off-Ramp Process

```
Crypto Wallet → Platform → Bank Account
```

1. Create transaction request
2. Wallet debited immediately
3. Crypto converted to fiat
4. Bank transfer initiated
5. Fiat arrives in bank account

**Total Time**: 1-2 business days

## Transaction Statuses

| Status | Color | Meaning | Your Action |
|--------|-------|---------|-------------|
| **Transaction Created** | Blue | Request initiated | Complete bank transfer (on-ramp) |
| **Waiting for Funds** | Amber | Awaiting your bank transfer | Transfer funds from your bank |
| **Processing** | Purple | Being processed | None - wait for completion |
| **Completed** | Green | Successfully finished | None - funds available |
| **Failed** | Red | Error occurred | Contact support |

<Info>
  You'll receive email notifications for each status change.
</Info>

## Before Creating Transactions

Ensure you have:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Business Approval">
    Your business must be approved (green badge in dashboard)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verified Bank Account">
    At least one bank account verified via email
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Active Crypto Wallet">
    At least one wallet created or verified
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sufficient Funds">
    **On-Ramp**: Funds available in bank account
    
    **Off-Ramp**: Crypto available in wallet
  </Step>
</Steps>

### On-Ramp Fees

- **Processing Fee**: 0.5%
- **Currency Tier**: 0.30% - 0.70% (based on currency and volume)

### Off-Ramp Fees

- **Processing Fee**: 1.0%

## Transaction Reference Codes

Every transaction gets a unique reference code:

- **On-Ramp**: Format `ON-XXXXXXXX`
- **Off-Ramp**: Format `OFF-XXXXXXXX`

<Warning>
  For on-ramps, you MUST include this reference code in your bank transfer. Without it, we cannot match your payment.
</Warning>

## Viewing Transactions

## Processing Times

### On-Ramp Timeline

```
0 min    - Transaction created
0-60 min - Complete bank transfer
5-120 min - Bank confirms transfer
60-120 min - Crypto sent to wallet
```

### Off-Ramp Timeline

```
0 min    - Transaction created, wallet debited
0-24 hrs - Crypto converted to fiat
24-48 hrs - Bank transfer initiated
24-48 hrs - Fiat arrives in bank account
```

<Tip>
  Bank transfers may be slower on weekends and holidays.
</Tip>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Start with small test transactions">
    Before processing large amounts, test with a small transaction (€100-500) to familiarize yourself with the process.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Double-check all details">
    Verify amounts, accounts, and reference codes before confirming. Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Keep transaction receipts">
    Save all bank transfer receipts and transaction confirmation emails for your records.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Monitor email notifications">
    Enable email notifications to stay updated on transaction status changes.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Plan for processing times">
    Factor in processing times when planning payments or settlements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
