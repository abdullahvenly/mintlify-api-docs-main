## What is an Off-Ramp?

An off-ramp converts cryptocurrency from your wallet into fiat currency in your bank account.

```
Your Crypto Wallet → Venly → Your Bank Account
```

<Info>
  Processing time: 1-2 business days for fiat to arrive in your bank
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before creating an off-ramp:

✅ Business approved (green badge)
✅ Verified bank account  
✅ Active crypto wallet with sufficient funds
✅ Crypto available in your wallet

## After Creating the Transaction

### Transaction Status Updates

**1. Transaction Created** (Blue)
- Request initiated
- Wallet will be debited
- **Your action**: None - automatic processing

**2. Processing** (Purple)
- Wallet debited
- Converting crypto to fiat
- Preparing bank transfer
- **Your action**: Wait for completion

**3. Completed** (Green)
- Bank transfer sent
- Fiat will arrive within 1-2 business days
- **Your action**: Check your bank account

### Email Notifications

You'll receive emails for:
- Transaction created
- Wallet debited
- Processing started
- Bank transfer initiated
- Fiat received (typically)

## Processing Timeline

```
Minute 0      - Create transaction
Minute 0      - Wallet debited immediately
Hour 0-24     - Crypto converted to fiat
Hour 24-48    - Bank transfer initiated
Day 1-2       - Fiat arrives in bank account
```

## Important Considerations

### Immediate Wallet Debit

<Warning>
  Your wallet is debited immediately when you confirm the transaction. Ensure you want to proceed before clicking "Confirm & Create".
</Warning>

### Bank Processing Times

- **Business days only**: Banks don't process on weekends/holidays
- **Same bank**: May arrive same day or next day
- **Different banks**: Typically 1-2 business days
- **International**: May take 2-3 business days

### Cannot Cancel

Once confirmed, off-ramp transactions cannot be cancelled as your wallet is immediately debited.

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Check Wallet Balance**: Ensure you have sufficient balance before creating the transaction.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Timing**: Create off-ramps early in the week (Mon-Wed) to avoid weekend delays.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Test Amount**: Start with a smaller amount for your first off-ramp.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Bank Details**: Verify your bank account details are correct before large off-ramps.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Fiat not arrived in 2 business days">
    **Check**:
    - Has it been 2 full business days (excluding weekends)?
    - Is your bank account active and not frozen?
    - Any notifications from your bank?
    
    **Solution**:
    1. Check transaction status in platform
    2. Verify with your bank
    3. Contact support@venly.io with transaction ID if delayed beyond 3 business days
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Wrong bank account selected">
    **Problem**: Selected wrong receiving account
    
    **Solution**:
    - Cannot change after confirmation
    - Fiat will go to selected account
    - Arrange internal transfer at your bank if needed
    - Contact support if account is no longer accessible
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed">
    **Reasons**:
    - Compliance issue detected
    - Bank account issue
    - Wallet balance insufficient (blockchain fees)
    
    **Solution**:
    - Check email for failure reason
    - Crypto will be returned to wallet
    - Contact support for clarification
    - Resolve issue and try again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Received less than expected">
    **Check**:
    - Exchange rate fluctuation (rate locked at confirmation)
    - Bank fees on receiving end
    - Currency conversion by your bank
    
    **Solution**:
    - Review transaction details for exact rate used
    - Check with your bank about their fees
    - Contact support if discrepancy is significant
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
