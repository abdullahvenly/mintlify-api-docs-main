## Adding a Bank Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    **Accounts** → **Bank Accounts** → **Whitelist Bank Account**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - **Account Name**: e.g., "Business Checking"
    - **IBAN**: Your International Bank Account Number
    - **Bank Name**: Full name of your bank
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify">
    Check email and click verification link (valid 24 hours)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Requirements

- Account must be in your business name
- IBAN format: Country code (2 letters) + digits
- Bank must be in a supported country

## Verification

**Timeline:** Email arrives within 2-10 minutes

**Didn't receive email?**
1. Check spam folder
2. Wait 10 minutes
3. Request new verification email
4. Contact support@venly.io

## Using Bank Accounts

**On-Ramp (Source):** Transfer fiat from your bank → Platform converts to crypto

**Off-Ramp (Destination):** Platform converts crypto → Fiat arrives in your bank

## Managing Accounts

- View all accounts in the Accounts page
- Edit account names
- Remove accounts (if no pending transactions)
- Add multiple accounts

<Tip>
  Add backup accounts in case of bank maintenance
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Invalid IBAN | Verify format, remove spaces |
| Failed verification | Check account is in business name, retry |
| Account shows failed | Review error, contact support |
