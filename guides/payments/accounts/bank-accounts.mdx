## Adding a Bank Account

Whitelist your business bank account to enable fiat transactions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Whitelist Dialog">
    1. Go to **Accounts** → **Bank Accounts** tab
    2. Click **Whitelist Bank Account** button
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Account Details">
    Fill in the required information:
    
    **Account Name**
    - Choose a descriptive name (e.g., "Business Checking")
    - Helps identify the account during transactions
    
    **IBAN**
    - Your bank account's International Bank Account Number
    - Format: 2-letter country code + check digits + account number
    - Example: `DE89370400440532013000`
    
    **Bank Name**
    - Full name of your financial institution
    - Example: "Deutsche Bank AG"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit for Verification">
    Click **Continue** to submit your details
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify via Email">
    1. Check your registered email inbox
    2. Look for verification email from Venly
    3. Click the verification link
    4. Account activates immediately after verification
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verification Process

### What to Expect

After submitting your bank account details:

**Immediate**: Confirmation screen appears with "Verification Email Sent" message

**Within 2-10 minutes**: Verification email arrives

**After clicking link**: Account status changes to "Verified"

<Info>
  Check your spam folder if you don't receive the email within 10 minutes.
</Info>

### Verification Email

The email contains:
- Verification link (valid for 24 hours)
- Account details for your review
- Next steps after verification

<Warning>
  Only click verification links from `@venly.io` email addresses. Never click suspicious links.
</Warning>

## Account Requirements

### Ownership

Bank accounts must:
- Be in your business name
- Match your registered business details
- Be from a supported country
- Be an active account

### Supported Countries

We support bank accounts from:
- European Union countries
- United Kingdom
- United States
- United Arab Emirates
- Singapore
- And more...

<Note>
  Contact support@venly.io to verify if your country is supported.
</Note>

## Managing Bank Accounts

### View Account Details

Each bank account card displays:
- Account nickname
- Masked IBAN (last 4 digits visible)
- Verification status badge
- Bank name

### Account Actions

Click the menu (⋮) on any account to:
- View full details
- Edit account name
- Remove account
- View transaction history

### Removing an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Account">
    Click the menu (⋮) on the account you want to remove
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Removal">
    Click **Remove Account** and confirm the action
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Removal">
    Account is immediately removed from your list
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  You cannot remove an account with pending transactions. Complete or cancel pending transactions first.
</Warning>

## Using Bank Accounts

### For On-Ramp Transactions

Your bank account is the **source** of funds:

1. Create an on-ramp transaction
2. Select your verified bank account
3. Receive bank transfer instructions
4. Complete the transfer from your bank
5. Wait for processing (1-2 hours)

[Learn more about on-ramp →](/guides/payments/transactions/on-ramp)

### For Off-Ramp Transactions

Your bank account is the **destination** for funds:

1. Create an off-ramp transaction
2. Select your verified bank account
3. Crypto is converted to fiat
4. Fiat arrives in 1-2 business days

[Learn more about off-ramp →](/guides/payments/transactions/off-ramp)

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invalid IBAN error">
    **Solution**:
    - Verify IBAN format is correct
    - Check for extra spaces or characters
    - Try entering without spaces
    - Verify IBAN belongs to your business
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Verification email not received">
    **Solution**:
    1. Check spam/junk folder
    2. Wait 10 minutes
    3. Add no-reply@venly.io to your contacts
    4. Request new verification email
    5. Contact support if still not received
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot add second bank account">
    **Solution**:
    - Verify first account before adding more
    - Ensure new account meets requirements
    - Check account is in your business name
    - Contact support for assistance
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Account shows 'Failed' status">
    **Solution**:
    - Review error message provided
    - Verify account details are correct
    - Ensure account is active with your bank
    - Try removing and re-adding the account
    - Contact support with error details
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Add Multiple Accounts**: Have backup accounts ready in case of bank maintenance or issues.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Descriptive Names**: Use clear names like "EUR Operations" or "USD Treasury" to identify accounts easily.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Verify Immediately**: Don't wait to verify accounts. Complete verification right after adding.
</Tip>

## Security Notes

### Bank Account Safety

- Only add accounts you own and control
- Never share banking credentials with anyone
- Use secure internet connections when adding accounts
- Enable 2FA on your email for added security

### Verification Links

- Links expire after 24 hours
- Only valid for one use
- Must be clicked from registered email
- Never share verification links

<Warning>
  Venly will never ask for your banking credentials, PIN, or online banking password.
</Warning>
