## Account Types

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Bank Accounts" icon="building">
    For fiat transactions (EUR, USD, GBP, etc.)
  </Card>
  <Card title="Crypto Wallets" icon="wallet">
    For digital asset transactions
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**You need both types** to use the platform:
- **On-Ramp**: Bank account → Crypto wallet
- **Off-Ramp**: Crypto wallet → Bank account

## Adding Accounts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Accounts">
    Click **Accounts** in the menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Type">
    Select **Bank Accounts** or **Wallets** tab
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add & Verify">
    - Bank: Enter IBAN, verify via email (2-10 min)
    - Managed Wallet: Created instantly
    - External Wallet: Send 1 USDC to verify (5-15 min)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Verified/Active** | Ready to use |
| **Verification in Process** | Awaiting confirmation |
| **Failed** | Retry or contact support |

## Supported Networks

## Supported Assets

**Stablecoins:** USDC, USDT, DAI, FRAX  
**Crypto:** ETH, MATIC

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Name Clearly**: Use descriptive names like "Business Checking" or "Treasury USDC"
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Start Small**: Add one of each type first, test with a small transaction
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Only add accounts you own and control. Never share private keys or seed phrases.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

**Email not received?** Check spam, wait 10 minutes, contact support

**Wallet not verifying?** Verify you sent exactly 1 USDC from the registered wallet

**Wrong balance?** Refresh page, check blockchain explorer
