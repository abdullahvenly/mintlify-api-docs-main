## Overview

## Stablecoin Transfers

**Flat Rate: 10 bps (0.10%)** for transfers without currency conversion (e.g., USDC to USDC)

## Transaction Fees

Fees based on:
1. **Currency tier** (determined by liquidity)
2. **Monthly volume** (higher volume = lower fees)

### Currency Tiers

| Tier | Currencies | $0-10M | $10M-50M | $50M+ |
|------|-----------|--------|----------|-------|
| **Tier 1** | USD, EUR, GBP | 20 bps (0.20%) | 15 bps (0.15%) | 10 bps (0.10%) |
| **Tier 2** | AED, SGD, HKD, MXN, BRL, PKR, ZAR | 30 bps (0.30%) | 25 bps (0.25%) | 20 bps (0.20%) |

## Gas Fees

Blockchain transaction fees (paid to validators, not Venly):

- **Polygon**: $0.01-$0.10 (1-10 bps on $100)
- **Ethereum**: $5-$50 (500-5000 bps on $100)
- **Avalanche**: $0.50-$2 (50-200 bps on $100)
- **BSC**: $0.20-$1 (20-100 bps on $100)

<Tip>
  **Use Polygon** for lowest gas fees - up to 500x cheaper than Ethereum
</Tip>
