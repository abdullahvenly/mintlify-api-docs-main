## Bank Accounts

### Adding a Bank Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    **Accounts** → **Bank Accounts** tab → **Whitelist Bank Account**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Account name
    - IBAN
    - Bank name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit for Review">
    Your bank account will be reviewed manually by the Venly team
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bank Account Requirements

- Account must be in your business name
- Valid IBAN format
- Bank must be in a supported country

### Bank Account Verification

**Process:** Manual review by Venly team

**Status:** "Verification in Process" → "Verified"

<Note>
  Bank account verification is done manually to ensure security and compliance
</Note>

## Crypto Wallets

### Whitelisting Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate">
    **Accounts** → **Wallets** tab → **Whitelist Wallet**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Wallet address (0x...)
    - Network (Ethereum or Polygon)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Ownership">
    You'll see a verification screen showing:
    - Amount: 1 USDC
    - Verification address
    
    Send exactly 1 USDC to the verification address **from the wallet you're registering**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Verification completes automatically once the blockchain confirms your transaction
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Must send 1 USDC from the exact wallet address you're registering
</Warning>

### Supported Networks & Assets

| Network | Assets |
|---------|--------|
| **Ethereum** | USDC, EURC, USDS, ETH |
| **Polygon** | POL |

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|

## Troubleshooting

### Bank Account Issues

**Account rejected?**
- Check IBAN is correct
- Ensure account is in business name
- Verify bank is in supported country

### Wallet Verification Issues

**Verification not completing?**
- Sent exactly 1 USDC?
- From the wallet you're registering?
- Correct network selected?
- Wait for blockchain confirmation

**Wrong network selected?**
- Cannot change after submission
- Must remove and re-add with correct network

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Name Clearly**: Use descriptive names like "Business Checking EUR" or "Treasury Wallet"
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Prepare During Processing**: Add accounts while your business verification is pending
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Never share** private keys or seed phrases
</Warning>
