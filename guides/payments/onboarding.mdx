## Verification Process

After registration, your business goes through a KYB (Know Your Business) verification to ensure compliance and security.

<Info>
  Verification typically takes 1-3 business days. You'll receive email updates throughout the process.
</Info>

## What We Verify

  <Card title="Business Registration" icon="building">
    Company legal status and registration details

## Verification Statuses

| Status | Timeline | Your Access |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Application in Process** | 1-3 days | Limited - can add accounts, explore features |
| **Approved** | After review | Full access to all features |

### While Pending

✅ **Available:** View dashboard, add bank accounts/wallets, review fees

❌ **Not Available:** Create transactions, use yield vaults

### After Approval

✅ **Full Access:** All features unlocked, ready to transact

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does verification take?">
    Typically 1-3 business days. Complex cases may take up to 5 business days.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What documents do I need?">
    Specific requirements are sent via email during verification. Generally: business registration, proof of address, and ID verification for owners.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if verification is rejected?">
    We'll email specific reasons and corrections needed. You can resubmit with the requested information.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
