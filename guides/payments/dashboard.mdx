## Key Metrics

### Business Status Badge

Located at the top right of your dashboard:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Application in Process">
    **Yellow Badge** indicates your business is under verification (1-3 days).
    
    Limited functionality until approval is complete.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Approved Business">
    **Green Badge** means you're fully approved and have access to all features.
    
    Ready to create transactions and use all platform capabilities.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Statistics Overview

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Volume" icon="trending-up">
    **What it shows**: Your total transaction volume over the last 30 days
    
    **Why it matters**: Determines your fee tier (higher volume = lower fees)
    
    **Example**: €248,391.00 with +12.5% growth trend
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Active Accounts" icon="wallet">
    **What it shows**: Number of verified bank accounts and crypto wallets
    
    **Breakdown**: Total count with split (e.g., "3 bank accounts, 5 wallets")
    
    **Action**: Click to manage your accounts
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Pending Requests" icon="clock">
    **What it shows**: Transactions awaiting processing
    
    **Statuses included**:
    - Transaction created
    - Waiting for funds
    - Processing
    
    **Action**: Click to view pending transactions
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Completed Transactions" icon="check-circle">
    **What it shows**: Successfully processed transactions
    
    **Breakdown**: Total with on-ramp/off-ramp split
    
    **Action**: Click to view transaction history
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Understanding Status Indicators

### Transaction Statuses

| Status | Color | Meaning | Next Steps |
|--------|-------|---------|------------|
| **Transaction Created** | Blue | Request initiated | Complete bank transfer |
| **Waiting for Funds** | Amber | Transfer pending | Wait for bank confirmation |
| **Processing** | Purple | Being processed | No action needed |
| **Completed** | Green | Successfully finished | Funds available |
| **Failed** | Red | Error occurred | Contact support |

### Account Statuses

| Status | Badge | Meaning | Action Required |
|--------|-------|---------|-----------------|
| **Verified** | Green | Active and ready | None |
| **Active** | Green | Wallet operational | None |
| **Verification in Process** | Amber | Awaiting confirmation | Check email or blockchain |
| **Pending** | Gray | Awaiting review | Wait for approval |

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with keyboard shortcuts:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `D` | Return to Dashboard |
| `A` | Go to Accounts |
| `T` | View Transactions |
| `N` | Create new transaction (when approved) |
| `/` | Focus search bar |

<Note>
  Keyboard shortcuts are available on desktop browsers only.
</Note>
