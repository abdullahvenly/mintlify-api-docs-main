## Platform Overview

Venly FundFlow Prime enables businesses to convert between fiat and crypto with enterprise-grade security.

## Onboarding Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="1. Receive Email">
    Get onboarding link via email from Venly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="2. Set Password & 2FA">
    Click link ‚Üí Set password ‚Üí Configure 2FA
  </Step>

## On-Ramp Flow

**Bank ‚Üí Venly ‚Üí Crypto Wallet**

1. Click **Create On-Ramp**
2. Enter fiat amount, select currencies
3. Choose bank account and wallet
4. Review and confirm
5. **Complete bank transfer with reference code**
6. Wait for crypto to arrive

## Off-Ramp Flow

**Crypto Wallet ‚Üí Venly ‚Üí Bank**

1. Click **Create Off-Ramp**
2. Enter crypto amount, select currencies
3. Choose wallet and bank account
4. Review and confirm
5. **Transfer crypto to Venly wallet**
6. **Add transaction hash**
7. Wait for fiat to arrive

## Key Requirements

**For Setup:**
- Onboarding email link
- 2FA authenticator app
- Bank account (IBAN)
- Crypto wallet address

**For Transactions:**
- Account verified
- Bank account verified
- Crypto wallet verified
- Sufficient funds

## Whitelisting

### Bank Account
1. Accounts ‚Üí Bank Accounts
2. Enter IBAN, bank name, account name
3. Submit for manual review

### Crypto Wallet
1. Accounts ‚Üí Wallets
2. Enter wallet address and network
3. Send 1 USDC from your wallet
4. Wait for confirmation

<Warning>
  Send 1 USDC from the exact wallet address you're registering
</Warning>
