## On-Ramp Flow (Buy Crypto)

**Bank ‚Üí Platform ‚Üí Crypto Wallet** | Processing: 1-2 hours

1. Click **Create On-Ramp**
2. Enter amount, select EUR/USD/GBP ‚Üí USDC/ETH/etc
3. Choose bank account and wallet
4. Confirm transaction
5. **Complete bank transfer with reference code** ‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL
6. Wait for crypto to arrive in wallet

**Fees:** 35-65 bps (0.35-0.65%) | 50%+ cheaper than competitors

## Off-Ramp Flow (Sell Crypto)

**Crypto Wallet ‚Üí Platform ‚Üí Bank** | Processing: 1-2 business days

1. Click **Create Off-Ramp**
2. Enter amount, select USDC/ETH/etc ‚Üí EUR/USD/GBP
3. Choose wallet and bank account
4. Confirm (wallet debited immediately)
5. Wait for fiat to arrive in bank

**Fees:** 60-90 bps (0.60-0.90%) | 60%+ cheaper than competitors
