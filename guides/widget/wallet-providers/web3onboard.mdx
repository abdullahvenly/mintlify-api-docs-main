## Overview

**Web3-Onboard** is one of the fastest and simplest method to incorporate multi-wallet and multi-chain support into your project. With pre-built modules supporting over 35 distinct hardware and software wallets, Web3-Onboard streamlines the process, saving you time and alleviating potential challenges.

### Installation:

```javascript
npm install @web3-onboard/core @web3-onboard/injected-wallets
```
